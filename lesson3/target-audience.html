Target Audience

The Target Audience will be lives in coummunities and it environs, business owners, entrepreneurs, investors, and torists who visit Kumasi city. It will also target students especially fresh graduates, familes and individuals. These Target Audience is going to provide development ideas, tools and financial advice to the people of the community. It is going to also seek the welfare of the city by seeking the interest of the city and knowing more about the history of the community.
