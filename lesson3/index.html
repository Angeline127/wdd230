Site Name

The name will be Kumasi Chamber of Commerce. I chose Kumasi because it is a city in my Country.

Site Purpose

Kumasi Chamber of Commerce site purpose will be to influence government policies and programmes, which will increase to the benefit of the private sector while promoting the overall development of the Ashanti Region's economy.

It will also seek to promote efficient and relaible business environment for the people of Kumasi and it environs. We pledge to bring nothing but the best businesses opportunities in the city.

Domain Name

The domain name for Kumasi Chamber of Commerce will be kumachamber.com
