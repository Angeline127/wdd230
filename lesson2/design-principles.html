Hick's Law

Pinterest

pinterest.com

I chose Pinterest for Hick's Laws because Hick's Law says that with every additional choice increases the time required to take a decision., it focuses on the narrow part of the design, it has fewer options for it users. Within few minutes the user can be able to find what they are looking within an ease. Pinterest is a common site that people normally search for ideas without enough filters
