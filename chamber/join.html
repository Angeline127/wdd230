Membership Levels

  • NP Membership

    This membership is a non-profit organization. As an NP member, you will pay zero fees for all activities and program your turn to organize. Meaning all fees will be free and you will enjoy full benefits like the other membership but there will conditions attached to it later in the future.

  • Bronze Membership

    As a Bronze Member, you will get 20% discount for 6 months training and 2 month mentorship. You will also have 20% on advertising and organizing materials for both your indoors and outdoors events. The annual cost of a Bronze membershp is $60.

  • Silver Membership

    As Silver Member, you will get 40% discount for 1 year training and 4 month mentorship. You will also have 40% on advertising and organizing materials for both your indoors and outdoors events. Also, you will be covered for a period of 6 months hotels expenses in any part of the country. The annual cost of a Silver membershp is $120.

  • Gold Membership

    As Gold Member, you will get 60% discount for 2 year training and 8 month mentorship. You will also have 60% on advertising and organizing materials for both your indoors and outdoors events. Also, you will be covered for a period of 11 months hotels expenses in any part of the country. Your employees are also going to receive a bonus worth $20 each if they exceed their monthly goals. The annual cost of a Gold membershp is $220.
